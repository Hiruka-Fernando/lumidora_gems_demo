Our Story of Radiance

For over three decades, we've been crafting stories of beauty, elegance, and timeless sophistication through our exceptional gemstones.

A Legacy Crafted in Brilliance

Founded in 1990 by master gemologist Elena Vance, Ceylon Lumidora Gems began as a small atelier with a simple yet profound vision: to bring the earth's most extraordinary treasures to those who appreciate true artistry.

What started as a passion project has grown into an internationally recognized house of fine gemstones, yet we've never lost sight of our founding principles. Every stone we select tells a story—of geological wonder, artisanal skill, and ethical sourcing.

Our journey has taken us from the sapphire mines of Kashmir to the emerald valleys of Colombia, always seeking stones with exceptional character and brilliance that deserve to be shared with the world.

Our Guiding Principles

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with certified mines and cooperatives that prioritize fair labor practices and environmental sustainability.

Uncompromising Quality

Each gem undergoes rigorous evaluation by our master gemologists to ensure exceptional color, clarity, and cut.

Artisanal Craftsmanship

Our jewelry is handcrafted by skilled artisans who blend traditional techniques with contemporary design.
